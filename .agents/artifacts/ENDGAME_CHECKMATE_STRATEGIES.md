## PART 5: COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### ❌ Mistake 1: Wasting aether early

**Problem:** Using DOUBLE_ATTACK (14 aether) on non-critical captures  
**Solution:** Save big powers for finishing blow  
**Strategy:** Use small powers (FROST 8, BLINK 8) for positioning

### ❌ Mistake 2: Not protecting attacker

**Problem:** Moving queen to attack square without SHIELD  
**Solution:** ALWAYS shield before attacking with expensive pieces  
**Strategy:** Queen attacked = dead, so SHIELD first (14 aether well spent)

### ❌ Mistake 3: Ignoring opponent's counter-threats

**Problem:** Going for mate while opponent threatens your king  
**Solution:** Clear opponent's attack first, THEN execute mate combo  
**Strategy:** Defend > Attack > Mate (in that order)

### ❌ Mistake 4: Leaving king exposed

**Problem:** Moving defensive pieces to attack  
**Solution:** Keep your own king safe while attacking  
**Strategy:** Never leave your king in check

### ❌ Mistake 5: Wrong timing for combo

**Problem:** Executing SHIELD+DOUBLE_ATTACK too early (position not ready)  
**Solution:** Wait until position forces checkmate in 2-3 moves  
**Strategy:** Save combo for when opponent king has no escape
