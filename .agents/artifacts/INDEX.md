## 💡 KEY INSIGHTS

✅ **Powers are NOT equal**
- Strategic powers >> Tactical powers
- Timing is EVERYTHING
- Right power, wrong phase = waste

✅ **Multipliers drive behavior**
- Small 1.5x → 4.0x changes eliminate whole mistake categories
- Graduated scales better than binary

✅ **Combos multiply value**
- FROST+IMPRISON > FROST OR IMPRISON
- Synergies create exponential gains

✅ **Game phases have different strategies**
- Opening: Develop & save
- Early Mid: Tactics & fountains
- Late Mid: Power combos & aggression
- Endgame: Spend or waste (no neutral option)
