================================================================================
                       KEY STRATEGIC INSIGHTS
================================================================================

GAME PHASES:
  Opening (1-10):         Develop pieces, accumulate aether passively
  Early Mid (11-15):      Power activation begins, fountain control contests
  Late Mid (16-25):       Tactical peak, expensive powers viable, cap pressure
  Endgame (26+):          Permanently capped, dump aether every turn, pawn races

AETHER ECONOMY:
  Optimal Band:           15-25 aether (sweet spot for flexibility)
  Cap Penalty:            30/30 = -3 aether/turn wastage (MUST spend)
  Fountain Value:         +2 aether/turn = 1 power cost every 7-9 turns
  Sacrifice Strategy:     Pawn (-100) → +6 aether (justified)

POWER SYNERGIES (Tier S):
  1. FROST + IMPRISON:    Freeze (8) → Next turn imprison (14) = Queen removal
  2. FORTIFY + DOUBLE:    Shield (14) → 2 captures (14) = guaranteed material
  3. AETHER_BLOCK + VNG:  Block (16) → Vengeance (18) = Queen destruction
  4. WALL + PROMOTE:      Wall (18) → Promote (15) = unbreakable fortress
  5. CLEANSE + DOUBL:     Cleanse (14) → 2 captures (14) = freed piece attacks
  6. IMPRISON + CAPTURE:  Imprison (14) → Next turn capture = undefended piece
  7. FROST + BLINK:       Freeze (8) → Blink (16) = safe escape
  8. CHRONOBREAK + UNDO:  Rewind (20) → Queen saved = game changer

DEFENSIVE TRIGGERS:
  ✓ FROST:                Opponent piece 300+ attacking, OR pawn 7th rank
  ✓ FORTIFY:              Our piece under 2+ attack, value >= 300
  ✓ CLEANSE:              Our Queen/Rook frozen or imprisoned
  ✓ WALL:                 King mating net, OR passed pawn protection
  ✓ BLINK:                Bomb threat (1-2 turns), OR attacked no escape
  ✓ CHRONOBREAK:          Opponent captured Queen/Rook (>400 material)

OFFENSIVE TRIGGERS:
  ✓ IMPRISON:             Our piece adjacent to opponent Queen/Rook
  ✓ DOUBLE_ATTACK:        2+ undefended pieces within reach
  ✓ PROMOTE:              Pawn 6th rank, opponent aether < 18
  ✓ VENGEANCE:            Opponent pawn 7th (emergency), OR Queen undefended
  ✓ AETHER_BLOCK:         Opponent 18+ aether + lethal move available
  ✓ BOMBA:                Opponent King in 3x3 blast zone + 2+ pieces

CRITICAL ANTI-PATTERNS TO AVOID:
  ❌ Frost on low-value pieces (<300 material)
  ❌ Promote without checking opponent Vengeance (18+ aether)
  ❌ Ignore passed pawn 7th rank (= -900 material loss)
  ❌ Chronobreak on minor losses (<400 material)
  ❌ Double Attack on defended pieces (recapture loss)
  ❌ Wall in sparse endgame (few pieces to justify 18 aether)
  ❌ AETHER_BLOCK when behind material (doesn't help)
  ❌ Let aether cap wastefully (= -3 aether/turn)

FOUNTAIN CONTROL:
  Value:                  +2 aether/turn (stacks with base)
  Break-Even:             10 turns of +2 = 1 mid-tier power
  Control Strategy:       Contest 50% of fountains to prevent acceleration
  Sacrifice ROI:          Pawn (-100) for fountain (+2/turn) justified if 10+ turns
